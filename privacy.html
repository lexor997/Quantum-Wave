Privacy first

We protect your information with the same care we secure your networks.

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we safeguard it when you work with Quantum Wave.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and organisation.
  • Technical information required to deliver services (system metadata, network diagrams, logs supplied for analysis).
  • Usage data from our support tools to improve responsiveness and service quality.

How we use data

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services and security posture.
  • Communicate about projects, incidents, billing, and service updates.
  • Develop new capabilities based on aggregated insights, never selling personal data.

Data protection

We implement layered security including encryption in transit, access controls, auditing, and secure data centres. Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or to meet legal obligations.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at info@quantumwave.co.nz to exercise your rights or ask questions.
