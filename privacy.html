Privacy

How Quantum Wave handles your data.

We keep client information minimal, secure, and only for the purposes of delivering and improving our services.

What we collect

  • Contact details you provide when you email or call us.
  • Operational data we access during projects to deliver services you request.
  • Website analytics that help us understand visits at an aggregate level.

Why we use it

  • To respond to enquiries, prepare proposals, and deliver projects.
  • To maintain and improve managed services and reliability for clients.
  • To meet legal, security, and compliance obligations.

How we protect it

  • Access controls and least-privilege for systems and documentation.
  • Encryption in transit and at rest for client materials we store.
  • Change control and audit trails for activities in client environments.

Your choices

  • Request access to or correction of personal information we hold.
  • Ask us to delete personal contact details we no longer need.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
